Guilds allow players to group together for new exploits and adventures.
Being in a guild bestows you a number of features;

-user buildable @home Servers on Grid, maintained by your members and visitable by anyone. Used as your guild headquarters and bestows new features.
-@home raiding & pvp between guilds
-@home VPN allowing users to hide their IP and immediately spawn on grid at their HQ
-Shared guild root folder for distributing custom software to your members
-Shared guild RAM allowing all members access to guild software when using their @home VPN
-Shared resource gathering
-Private chat relay on subnet (makes pms harder to track & crack)
-guild key for shared message & file encryption
-requires 2 members to form (one admin, one manager)
-additional members online increase @home CPU by 1 core
